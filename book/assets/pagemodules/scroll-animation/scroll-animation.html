Oil Filter Change
Refresher Training

-1-
Ensure the engine is switched off and the area around the oil filter canister is clean from debris. When ready, remove the oil filter canister and discard the used filter element.
-2-
Inspect and prepare the new oil filter (SP1) and seal. When ready, screw on the new oil filter - do not over tighten, then wipe clean any spilt oil.
-3-
Carry out the electrical power-up procedure and check the vehicle fluid levels.
-4-
Do the required conditions before power-up procedure. When ready, start the engine and let it run at low idle speed for two minutes.
-5-
Check for leakage from the oil filter assembly.
-6-
Stop the engine and check the oil level on the dipstick. When necessary, add more oil, start the engine and run let it at low idle speed for two minutes.
-7-
Repeat Step 6.
Scroll up and down, or use the arrow keys, to control the animation.
You have now completed the oil filter change refresher training.
