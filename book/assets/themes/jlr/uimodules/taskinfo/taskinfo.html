Task Information

Safety Conditions

  • WARNING: Scalding Hazard and Danger of Burns. Hot oil and hot components can cause personal injury. Do not allow hot oil or hot components to contact the skin.
  • WARNING: Hot Surfaces. The engine and associated components run at very high temperatures. Take extreme care when working near these hot components.
  • WARNING: Toxic Hazard - Oil. When working with lubricating and other oils:
    1. Always wear suitable protective equipment to prevent contact with the skin.
    2. Never eat, drink or smoke until clear of the area and have been cleansed of oil contamination.
    3. Never carry oil-contaminated items in pockets.
